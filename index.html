Stock Picking Framework
After months of researching stocks, I've settled on a framework that works for me...

After months of researching stocks, I've settled on a framework that works for me: focus on FCF yield, management alignment, and catalysts.

FCF yield is the clearest signal of value. A business that generates real cash, not accounting profits. Management alignment means they own stock and are incentivized the same way I am. Catalysts are what turn a good idea into a profitable trade.

The hardest part isn't finding ideas — it's waiting for the right price. Patience is the edge.
